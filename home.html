Welcome to Pro Martial Arts. Northampton’s Premier Family Martial Arts Centre

We specialise in Lau Gar Kung Fu, Kickboxing and Fitness. Our mission is to help you achieve your goals by developing your mind, body, and spirit to reach your full potential. Our dedicated team of Lau Gar Guardian and Senior Instructors are committed to making your training experience rewarding by providing a first class, multi-purpose training facility and most importantly giving you the finest tuition available with each of them having nearly 30 years experience.

Whether your aim is to achieve good health and fitness or become an elite black belt; we have the program to support and assist your growth and progress plan. We continually encourage individuals and families through our work in the local community to lead healthier lifestyles. Learning Lau Gar Kung Fu and Kickboxing is a true-life skill beneficial to people of all ages and abilities.

What is Lau Gar Kung Fu?

Lau Gar Kuen is the style’s full name. In English it means:
Lau – The family name (Lau)
Gar – Family
Kuen – Fist

About us

We are a family-orientated martial arts club who are open to all ages, abilities and fitness levels.
Our foundation is Lau Gar Kung Fu, and we are affiliated with the British Kung Fu Association.
This gives us the basis to build on our kickboxing techniques using many of the traditional methods.
Pro Martial Arts was founded by our Chief Instructor Tracy Edghill, and officially opened in March 2013 by Master Yau, the keeper of the Lau Gar style.
Tracy had a wealth of knowledge in the health and fitness industry having been a personal instructor and trainer for over 20 years, but his experience and energy came from his passion for martial arts.” Sadly, we lost our friend and mentor in January 2014, and he leaves a big void in the lives of family and friends. His devotion to the martial arts world is an inspiration to us all. Our Lau Gar Guardian Robert Francis (5th Degree Black Belt), alongside our senior instructors Chris Collymore (3rd Degree Black Belt), Neville Palmer (3rd Degree Black Belt) and with the help of our 1st Degree Black Belt instructor Jamaine Brown continue Tracy’s legacy and dream at Pro Martial Arts.

Kick Box

Kick Boxing - Although the syllabus, laid down by Master Yau, concentrates to a large extent on the traditional side of martial arts the style has produced many Kickboxing, World, European and National Champions in Semi Contact, Light Contact and Full Contact.
Kickboxing is a sport and uses several basic techniques derived from the traditional side of martial arts. It has become a very popular form of training in the UK and throughout the World.
It is also very popular amongst people of all ages.
It is an effective system of training that gets people punching, kicking, blocking, and sparring in a short period of time.
